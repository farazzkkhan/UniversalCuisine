FOR CHICKEN BALLS
  • 500gm Chicken leg or breast
  • Spring onion greens, finely chopped
  • Carrots, shredded
  • 1 tsp White pepper powder
  • 2 CHicken stock cubes (crumbled)
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • 2 1/2 tbsp Ginger, grated
  • 1 Egg (for binding)
  • to taste salt
  • A pinch of star anise powder
  • 2 Cloves Garlic, chopped
  • 3 tbsp Soya sause
  • Potato starch (opitional)
FOR THE SOUP
  • 5 Cups chicken broth or predicament mixed with 2 stock cubes
  • 1/2 cup mushroom, chopped
  • few spinach leaves (cleaned and kept aside)
  • 3 lemons (juiced if possible)
  • soya sauce
  • to taste salt
  • 2 eggs (beaten)
INSTRUCTIONS:
  1. Mix all ingredients above with mince till evenly distributed and place in the refigerator.
PREPARE THE SOUP
  • Heat stock when boiling add the egg mix till it scrambles within the soup. Mix lightly
  • take chicken out of the fridge and form balls with wet hands.
  • Add to soup. Now relish the soup and modify the seasoning with salt and siya sauce
  • Finally, add mushrooms and shredded carrots.
  • Let it cook till they need a pleasent crunch and not overdone
  • Add lemon juice and taste.
  • If you need more juice, add. Don't overcook the lemon juice.
  • In the end. just before serving, throw in the bean sprouts and torn spinach leaves and serve.
  • Serve with vinegar-chillies and your favourite chilli sause.
