INGREDIENTS FOR AMARNATH TIKIS
  • 1 cup amarnath (chaulai) seeds
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, finely minced
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 1/2 cup finely chopped zucchini
  • 1/2 cup finely chopped red bell pepper
  • salt, to taste
  • 1/4 tbsp chilli powder
  • a pinch of amchoor
  • 1 tbsp garam masala
  • 1/2 tsp coriander powder
  • 1/2 tsp lemon zest
  • lots of freshly ground blaack pepper
  • 2 tbsp oil, too pan fry
  • amarnath flour or breadcrumbs for dusting
COOKING THE AMARNATH SEEDS
  • boil 2 cups of water and add the amarnath stiring well
  • after a quick boil, cover the bowl, lower the heat and let it simmer for 20 minutes.
  • when amarnath is cooked, season with sea salt and freshly ground pepper.keep aside.
FOR THE MASALA
  • In a small pan, add 1 tbsp oil, saute garlic and onion until glossy.
  • then add the red pepper and zucchini, stirfry for 2 to 3 minutes.
  • then add the lemon zest, garam masala powder, coriander powder, red chilli, and stir fry. add the amchoor and black pepper. remove from heat and set aside
  • add this mixture to the boiled amarnath. let it cool until easily handled
  • then make patties. coat them in amarnath flour or breadcrumbs.
  • pan fry them, using 2 tablespoons of oil till golden.
